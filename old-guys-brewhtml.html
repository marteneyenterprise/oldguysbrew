Our Story

Two Worlds Under
One Roof

⬥

Old Guys Brew is Pawnee City's one-of-a-kind hangout — a coffee shop and saloon rolled into one. By day, we're your neighborhood coffeehouse serving up hot and cold espresso drinks in a warm, laid-back atmosphere. As the evening rolls in, the saloon side comes alive with cold beers, cocktails, and the kind of wild-west charm that makes you feel right at home.

Owner Chuck Rathbun (who also runs Willard's Tavern in Steinauer) built this place with a simple idea: great coffee and great company don't have to be separate. Come in for your morning cup or stay for the night — we've got you covered either way.

The Experience

Why You'll Love It Here

⬥
01

Morning to Midnight

Start your day with a perfectly brewed cup of coffee, then come back in the evening when the saloon lights up. Whatever time it is, there's always a seat for you at Old Guys Brew.

02

Wild West Vibes

The saloon side is decked out in genuine wild west memorabilia and displays — a nod to the history and spirit of small-town Nebraska that makes you feel like you've stepped back in time.

03

Community at Heart

From weekend brunches to live entertainment and local events, Old Guys Brew has quickly become Pawnee City's favorite gathering spot — where everybody knows your name.
